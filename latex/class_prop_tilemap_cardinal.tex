\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Handles tiling on paths or other tiling props. Each material in tile\+Materials is associated with a "{}tile"{} that is placed only when certain connected\+Tile conditions are met. 

MESHES (All should be facing North / North\+East when possible!) 0\+: No connections 1\+: One connection 2\+: Two Connections (Straight Line) 3\+: Three Connections (T-\/\+Junction) 4\+: Two Connections (Corner) 5\+: All Connections (Cross)
